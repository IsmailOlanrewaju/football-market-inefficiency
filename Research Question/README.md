## Research Question

The central research question of this project is:

**To what extent are football transfer market valuations of young players explained by measurable player characteristics rather than reputation-based factors?**

To investigate this, the analysis explores the following questions:

- How well can observable player characteristics explain current market valuations?
- What role does club reputation play in determining player value?
- Do market valuations differ significantly from values suggested by statistical models?
- Are valuation differences consistent across different playing positions?

By addressing these questions, this project aims to provide insight into how efficiently the football transfer market prices young talent and the extent to which non-performance factors influence player valuation.
